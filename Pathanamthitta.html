CHUTTIPPARA

Chuttippara, a 200 ft. rock cluster is one of the tourist spots where you can get a panoramic view of Pathanamthitta town. Atop the hill, there is a temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, Harihara Mahadeva Temple. Prominent hill above Pathanamthitta with a lookout point offering city & sunset views.
